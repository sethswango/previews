Getting found on Google - quick wins we spotted

For a future round - nothing here is in this release

The goal: when someone in Springfield searches “marketing agency” or “branding agency,” Kidsister should be one of the names they see. Those searches currently show several other local agencies, and Kidsister isn’t in the results yet.

This round fixed the basics: every page has a real name Google can read, and new case study pages are no longer skipped. That doesn’t move rankings by itself.

The next three, in payoff order:

  • Google Business Profile. Free listing that puts you in the map box and shows your reviews. The biggest single win.
  • Say “Springfield” where it counts. The site barely uses the words people type into Google.
  • Make the contact page load fast. Slow pages rank lower and lose people.

Can scope this as its own round if you want it.
